About Asunaro Works

Asunaro Works reimagines background art, AI-generated visuals, and interactive elements as a form of **story-driven art**.
By weaving light, atmosphere, and subtle motion through code, we explore ways for viewers to experience artwork not just as images, but as **immersive moments**.

Main Activities

  • Ongoing AI art exhibition series: PrismCat Memory
  • Background art assets available on Itch.io
  • Technical notes & research articles published on Note
  • Visual stories such as Renewal of the Forest Covenant and Usako & Kameko available on BOOTH and Itch.io
  • Character Risuko creator stickers available on LINE STORE
