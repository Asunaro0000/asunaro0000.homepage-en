About

Asunaro Works focuses on storyboard production, creating background art, scene composition, and visual expression.
Beyond the completeness of a single illustration, emphasis is placed on conveying story and atmosphere through the continuity and flow of images.

With screen design centered on storytelling elements such as the relationship between background and characters, visual guidance, and the use of pauses and timing, storyboards are created to suit purposes including educational use, narrative expression, and presentation or exhibition content.

Depending on the project requirements, production that includes character design is also available.

Main Activities and Achievements

  • Creation and publication of original storyboard works
  • Production and sale of background art and background materials
  • Creation of visual poetry and narrative expression works
  • Writing note articles to document production processes and techniques
  • Development of original character-related content
  • Awarded in the AI Halloween category, magazine cover art
    Featured on the cover of AICU JAPAN 'AICU Magazine Vol.17'

Production Environment and Tools

ComfyUI is primarily used for production, with workflows built specifically for storyboards and background expression.
After generation, image editing software is used for retouching and adjustments through inpainting, refining the final output with attention to visual consistency and information organization.
